defense Calculator

The defense Calculator asks you quesitons about the castle being attacked, and the incoming army. It's faily straightforward. Enter in the information about your catle, and the corresponding information from the attack warning

Enter the tools you've got to defend yourself, and press the "Calculate" button. If you get everything right, the answer will be correct. Credits to Phil for the logic and scripts.
